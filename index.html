Answers to your most pressing questions

To escape the police, you need to break their line of sight and stay hidden until your wanted level decreases. Use alleyways, tunnels, and parking garages to hide. Changing vehicles also helps throw them off your trail.
Yes, the game features three difficulty levels: Rookie (easy), Fugitive (medium), and Most Wanted (hard). Higher difficulties feature smarter AI, more police units, and tougher roadblocks.
Currently, Escape Car is a single-player experience. However, we're developing a multiplayer mode where you can either cooperate with friends in escapes or compete against each other in police evasion challenges.
Your wanted level (1-5 stars) increases with each crime committed. At 1 star, only local police pursue you. At 5 stars, you'll face SWAT teams, helicopters, and military roadblocks. The level decreases when you avoid detection.
Yes, you can find hidden garages throughout the city where you can repair your vehicle for a price. Alternatively, you can steal another car to continue your escape with a fresh vehicle.
